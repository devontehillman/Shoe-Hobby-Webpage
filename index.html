My Shoe Addiciton

It all started with my next door neighbors mom's shoe collection. I would walk into their house and see every jordan colorway under the sun. I always thought to my self "some day I'll have that pair... and that pair... and that pair". As I got older and started to work I could then afford to buy the shoes I wanted. I wouldn't think of it as an addiction but more of a passion. Not ony do I collect shoes (primarily jordans), I also restore and customize them.
